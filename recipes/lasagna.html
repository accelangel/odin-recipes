Mamma's Lasagna

This classic italiano meal is loaded with layers of cheese and meat and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Provoline, Mozzarella and Parmesan
Steps
  1. Go to loblaws
  2. In the frozen section, grab a lasagna
  3. Pay for it and go home
  4. Put the lasagna in the microwave for 8 minutes
  5. Let it cool for 3 minutes before digging in
