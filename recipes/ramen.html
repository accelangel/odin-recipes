Instant ramen

This classic asian meal is loaded with noodles, meat and a savory broth.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • broth packet
  • veggie packet
Steps
  1. Go to loblaws
  2. In the soup section, grab a package of ramen
  3. Pay for it and go home
  4. Empty the broth and veggie packet into the ramen cup
  5. Pour boiling water into the ramen cup and wait 6 minutes
  6. Mix the noodles and enjoy!
